## Selected Design: Swiss Modernism + Editorial Tech

Chosen for its intellectual confidence, strong typographic hierarchy, and timeless editorial feel — perfect for a CS student who wants to convey both technical depth and professional polish. The amber accent adds warmth without being playful, and the off-white background ensures excellent readability.
